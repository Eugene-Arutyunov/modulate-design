About Modulate

Modulate is a frontier voice Al lab which developed a new kind of Al model for voice understanding that's 100x faster, cheaper, and more accurate than LLMs. Built on our history in the gaming world, understanding highly nuanced and emotional conversations, our models can recognize subtle hints like sarcasm and truly understand the meaning behind a conversation.

Our proprietary model is now available to directly power voice Al agents, compliance software, and improved conversational analytics, while our platform allows businesses to seamlessly access this intelligence without need of costly and difficult prompting or retraining.
