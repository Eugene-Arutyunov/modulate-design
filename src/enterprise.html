The platform

Our platform allows businesses to seamlessly access this intelligence without need of costly and difficult prompting or retraining.

Real-time processing

Analyze conversations as they happen with sub-second latency. Our ELM architecture processes audio streams instantly, enabling immediate detection of behaviors, emotions, and compliance issues without interrupting the conversation flow or requiring post-processing delays.

Conversation fingerprint

Visualize everything happening between the lines with our unique conversation fingerprint technology. See emotional patterns, behavioral markers, and nuanced interactions in an intuitive dashboard that makes complex conversation data immediately understandable and actionable.

API integration

Integrate Modulate's intelligence into your existing systems with our comprehensive REST API and SDKs. No complex prompting or model retraining required—simply send audio streams and receive rich behavioral insights that work seamlessly with your current infrastructure.

Custom behavior detection

Define and detect behaviors specific to your industry or use case without retraining models. Our platform allows you to configure custom detection rules, thresholds, and behavioral patterns that align with your organization's unique requirements and compliance needs.

Analytics dashboard

Access powerful analytics and reporting tools that transform raw conversation data into actionable insights. Track trends, monitor compliance metrics, identify patterns across thousands of interactions, and generate comprehensive reports that help drive business decisions.

Enterprise security

Built with enterprise-grade security and compliance standards. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with SOC 2 compliance, GDPR readiness, and comprehensive audit logs. Deploy on-premises or in your preferred cloud environment with full data sovereignty control.

Use cases

Our platform can be used for a wide range of use cases, including:

Customer service

Analyze customer interactions in real-time to identify sentiment, detect frustration, and ensure quality service delivery. Our ELM understands nuanced emotions and can flag conversations that need immediate attention, helping teams provide better support and improve customer satisfaction.

Fraud detection

Detect suspicious patterns and fraudulent behavior in voice conversations through advanced behavioral analysis. Our models recognize subtle cues like stress, deception, and manipulation tactics that traditional systems miss, enabling faster identification of fraud attempts before they cause damage.

Compliance

Monitor conversations automatically to ensure adherence to regulatory requirements and industry standards. HR analytics

Analyze interview conversations and employee interactions to gain insights into communication patterns and team dynamics. Understand candidate fit, identify potential issues in workplace conversations, and track engagement levels to make data-driven decisions about hiring and team management.

Legal compliance

Automatically review and flag conversations that may violate legal requirements or company policies. Internal investigations

Quickly search and analyze recorded conversations to identify relevant information during internal reviews. Our platform enables investigators to find specific behaviors, emotional patterns, or compliance issues across thousands of hours of recordings, dramatically reducing investigation time and improving accuracy.
